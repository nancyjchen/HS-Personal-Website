Omni-Plan

For the chaotic, messy, and spontaneous.

Designed by planners, organizers, and arrangers.

The Spark: Making my college list on a spreadsheet was a pain. But what if there was a table that automatically fills in the data I need (i.e. SAT/ACT range, acceptance rate)?
Likewise, what if there was a trip planner that calculates the time and distance between locations for me? Omni-Plan is designed to solve these problems.
